MILK

Begin pouring from a few centimeters above the cup so the milk sinks under the crema. Once the cup is partially full, lower the pitcher close to the surface and pour a rosetta pattern for the body of the swan by moving the pitcher gently side to side while moving forward. As you reach the top of the cup, lift the pitcher slightly and pour a small heart or teardrop shape to form the swan’s head. Finally, draw a thin line from the head through the body to create the neck, giving the design its elegant swan shape. Precision, steady hand movements, and consistent microfoam are essential to executing this design cleanly.
